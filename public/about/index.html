About

I am a B.S. Graduate in Cybersecurity from Fudan University (College of Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence). Currently, I work as a Full-Stack Software Engineer at SAP and a Remote Research Intern at MOE Key Lab of Computational Linguistics, Peking University.

My research interests lie in Mechanistic Interpretability and LLM Reasoning. I have been fortunate to work with Prof. Liangming Pan (Peking University) and Prof. Yixin Cao (Fudan University) on understanding how LLMs perform logical reasoning.
