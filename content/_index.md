## About Me

I am a **B.S. Graduate** in **Cybersecurity** from [Fudan University](https://www.fudan.edu.cn/) (College of Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence). Currently, I work as a **Full-Stack Software Engineer** at [SAP](https://www.sap.com/) and a **Remote Research Intern** at [MOE Key Lab of Computational Linguistics, Peking University](https://cs.pku.edu.cn/).

My research interests lie in **Mechanistic Interpretability** and **LLM Reasoning**. I have been fortunate to work with [Prof. Liangming Pan](https://scholar.google.com/citations?hl=zh-CN&user=JcjjOTUAAAAJ) (Peking University) and [Prof. Yixin Cao](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=CnhTvdoAAAAJ&hl=zh-CN&oi=ao) (Fudan University) on understanding how LLMs perform logical reasoning.
