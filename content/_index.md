## Danchun Chen 陈丹纯

<p class="email">dcchen.nexus [at] gmail [dot] com</p>

I'm a Full-Stack Engineer at [SAP](https://www.sap.com/) and a Research Intern at [Peking University](https://cs.pku.edu.cn/), advised by [Prof. Liangming Pan](https://liangmingpan.github.io/). I received my B.S. in Cybersecurity from [Fudan University](https://www.fudan.edu.cn/) in 2025.

My research focuses on **Mechanistic Interpretability** and **LLM Reasoning** — understanding how language models perform reasoning tasks by reverse-engineering their internal computations.

Previously, I was a Research Engineer at Fudan University working with [Prof. Yang Zhou](https://faculty.fudan.edu.cn/zhouyang1/en/) on LLM applications from September 2024 to June 2025.
