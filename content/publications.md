**Towards a Mechanistic Understanding of Propositional Logical Reasoning in Large Language Models**

**Danchun Chen**<sup>&#42;</sup>, Qiyao Yan<sup>&#42;</sup>, Liangming Pan

*arXiv preprint 2026*

- Mechanistic analysis on four models (Qwen3-8B/14B, Llama-3.1-8B-Instruct, Mistral-7B-Instruct) using *PropLogic-MI* across 11 rule categories and one-/two-hop reasoning.
- Uncovered four interlocking mechanisms: Staged Computation, Information Transmission, Fact Retrospection, and Specialized Attention Heads.
- Prompt-order control analysis confirms the staged organization is model-internal.
